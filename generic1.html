Post Covid-19 Analysis

Project Date: Feb,2024 - Personal Project

Introduction

2023 sees a cautious hope in the COVID-19 saga. While the pandemic persists, global data reveals a decline in cases, deaths, and hospitalizations compared to late 2022. Drops of 58% in cases and 31% in deaths paint a promising picture, but vigilance remains key. JN.1, the dominant variant, is under control, but equity in healthcare and future mutations warrant caution. This data-driven snapshot, built on WHO records and your SQL analysis, offers a crucial understanding of the evolving landscape, reminding us that the pandemic isn't over, but the corner might be in sight.

Objective

  • What is the trend of Covid 19 in 2023?
  • What is the percentage of the world population that has been Infected?
  • What the outlook of Covid 19 in each Continent?
  • What the outlook of Covid 19 in ASEAN countries?

Data Source

https://ourworldindata.org/covid-deaths

Method Used

Basic Data Retrieval, Basic Calculation, Table Join, Rolling Total, Common Table Expression (CTE) & Temporary Table.
