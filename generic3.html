Movie Industry

Project Date: Feb,2024 - Personal Project

Introduction

Is the movie industry dying? is Netflix the new entertainment king? Those were the first questions that lead me to create a dataset focused on movie revenue and analyze it over the last decades. But, why stop there? There are more factors that intervene in this kind of thing, like actors, genres, user ratings and more.There are 6820 movies in the dataset (220 movies per year, 1986-2016) and each movie has the their following attributes.

Objective

  • Is there any correlation between movie and their production team?
  • What is the most significant correlation between movie and its attribute?

Data Source

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/danielgrijalvas/movies

Method Used

Google Colab, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Numpy
